About us

Life Travel started out back in 1979 with just one mission: to create affordable adventures for awesome humans. Since then we've launched apps, award-winning film projects, digital music platforms and pioneered charity campaigns. We've crossed borders to push boundaries, walked unfamiliar paths to find new friends, and got lost to discover the places we love. We've done all these things, but our philosophy has never changed. We're still the place to #StartTheAdventure. And now, it's your turn.

Our vision

To become one of the top organizations in the tourism industry with local, regional and international recognition for our high standards, solid organization and respect to whom we are and what we do.

Few words About us

We're not just about students, we're about seeing the world for less. So whatever your age or adventure, we guarantee to have the lowest prices on scheduled airfares, tours and accommodation.
